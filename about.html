About Calmverse

What is Calmverse?

Calmverse is a web app that host calming games and provides mental health resources.

Why is it named Calmverse and what is the concept?

Calmverse is a combination of calm and universe. The concept is there are multiple calm universe that specializes in a variety of emotions and mental states.

How does Calmverse works?

The user will take the quiz and then choose the choice which best represents their feelings and mental state. Each choice will direct the user to a calm universe that specializes in the user's choice of emotion with a selection of games.
